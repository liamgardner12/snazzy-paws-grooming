Pet Care Education: Why Grooming Matters

Regular grooming isn't just about looking good; it's vital for your dog's health. Here are three reasons why you should keep a regular schedule:

  • Skin Health: Bathing removes dirt and irritants that can cause hot spots or infections.
  • Early Detection: Our groomers check for lumps, bumps, or parasites that you might miss at home.
  • Comfort: De-matting and nail trimming prevent pain and mobility issues.

We recommend a professional groom every 4 to 6 weeks depending on your dog's breed and coat length.
